How to Use Cookie Jar

Your complete guide to building a personal motivation vault

Cookie Jar is inspired by David Goggins' mental technique of storing your wins, motivational moments, and powerful words in a mental "cookie jar" that you can reach into when life gets tough.

Our app takes this concept digital ‚Äî letting you save affirmations, tweets, and YouTube videos that you can revisit whenever you need motivation.

The concept is simple: When you're at your best, fill the jar. When you're struggling, pull a cookie and remind yourself who you are.

Cookie Jar is available exclusively on iOS. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Download

Download Cookie Jar from the App Store and start using it right away.

Step 2: Onboarding

Swipe through the onboarding screens to understand the core features: saving content and pulling random cookies when you need motivation.

‚úçÔ∏è Adding Content to Your Jar

There are three types of content you can save:

1. Text Affirmations

Write personal affirmations, motivational quotes, or reminders to yourself.

  • Tap "Add New" from the home screen
  • Select "Text" as the content type
  • Write your affirmation
  • Save it to your jar

üí° Affirmation Ideas

"I've overcome harder things than this." ‚Ä¢ "I am capable, strong, and resilient." ‚Ä¢ "This challenge is making me stronger."

2. Tweet Links

Save inspiring tweets before they disappear from your timeline.

  • Copy the tweet URL from Twitter/X
  • Tap "Add New" and select "Tweet"
  • Paste the URL
  • Add a note about why it inspires you

3. YouTube Videos

Keep motivational speeches, interviews, and videos that light your fire.

  • Copy the YouTube video URL
  • Tap "Add New" and select "YouTube"
  • Paste the URL
  • Add a description to remember why you saved it

üî• Pull a Cookie

The magic happens when you need motivation. Tap the big cookie on the home screen to pull a random piece of content from your jar.

  • Text affirmations open in the player with AI voice playback
  • Tweets open directly in Twitter/X
  • YouTube videos open in the YouTube app

The randomness is intentional ‚Äî you never know what you need to hear until you hear it. Trust the jar.

üíª Accessing on Laptop or Desktop

Cookie Jar is currently a mobile-first iOS app. However, there are ways to access your motivation on larger screens:

Option 1: iPhone Mirroring (macOS Sequoia)

If you have macOS Sequoia, you can mirror your iPhone directly to your Mac:

  • Ensure your iPhone and Mac are signed into the same Apple ID
  • Open the "iPhone Mirroring" app on your Mac
  • Your iPhone screen appears on your Mac
  • Use Cookie Jar as if you were on your phone

Option 2: iPad with Sidecar

If Cookie Jar becomes available on iPad, you can use Sidecar to extend your Mac display:

  • Connect your iPad as a second display
  • Run Cookie Jar on the iPad portion
  • Keep motivation visible while working

Option 3: Export Your Affirmations

For desktop access to your written affirmations:

  • Keep a note in Apple Notes or Notion synced across devices
  • Copy your favorite affirmations there for desktop access
  • Use the app for the voice playback experience on mobile
