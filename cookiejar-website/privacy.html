Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Cookie Jar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data when you use our mobile application.

We Do Not Collect Data

Cookie Jar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your content—including affirmations, saved tweets, and YouTube videos—is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, or third-party services that collect information about you or your usage of the app.

No Account Required

Cookie Jar does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Local Storage Only

All data you create in Cookie Jar remains on your device. We have no access to your content, and it is never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, Cookie Jar is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
