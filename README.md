# Cookie Jar - Landing Page

Landing page for Cookie Jar, a personal motivation app inspired by David Goggins' mental technique of storing your wins and motivational moments.

## About Cookie Jar

Cookie Jar lets you save affirmations, tweets, and YouTube videos that inspire you. When life gets tough, pull a random "cookie" from your jar to remind yourself who you are.

## Features

- Save personal affirmations and motivational quotes
- Store inspiring tweets before they disappear
- Keep YouTube videos that light your fire
- Pull random cookies when you need a boost

## Pages

- `index.html` - Main landing page
- `guide.html` - User guide and FAQ
- `privacy.html` - Privacy policy
- `terms.html` - Terms of service

## Development

To run locally:

```bash
cd cookiejar-website
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

Then open http://localhost:8000 in your browser.

## Deployment

The site is configured for Vercel deployment with `vercel.json`.
